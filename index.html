Our Story

It all started on a rainy Tuesday in a small coffee shop. Michael, a software engineer, was sketching out a new app idea when he noticed Sarah, a graphic designer, struggling to find a power outlet. A simple offer to share the outlet led to a conversation that lasted for hours.

From that day forward, their shared love for bad puns, adventurous hiking trails, and late-night movie marathons blossomed into a deep and beautiful connection. Four years, two cats, and countless memories later, Michael proposed on the very hiking trail where they had their first date. We are so excited to start our next chapter together, and we can't wait to celebrate with all of you!
